About my school

Education is the Path to Enlightened Life” Gazipur Cantonment Public School and College commenced its journey in 2017 with this greatest motto of education as instructed by the Chief Patron, Commandant at Bangladesh Ordnance Factories (BOF). It is situated in a charming environment inside the cantonment area of Bangladesh Ordnance Factories. Gazipur Cantonment Public School and College started its glorious journey with 9 classes from Nursery to Class VII with 3 sections in each. Both Bangla and English versions started with a good number of students. We are currently having around 2500 students and 90 teachers in our institution. Further infrastructural and academic development are going on. Gazipur Cantonment Public School and College has an impressive multistoried modern building. The building provides all the modern learning facilities like well-furnished classrooms, spacious play zone, well-arranged conference hall, labs, a library, canteen and ample facilities for the students’ entertainment, cultural and co-curriculum activities. The canteen provides healthy and hygienic foods to the students. There is also a pure water supply with Reverse Osmosis System on every floor. An army officer is the Principal of the institution. The governing body consists of renowned civil and defence officials. The teaching staff includes experienced, talented and expert teachers. The front office is the main contact point for all visitors & parents and is open from 7:30 am-4:00 pm, Sunday-Thursday. Gazipur Cantonment Public School and College is always ready to welcome any kind of suggestion from its well-wishers, parents and partners. We nourish the talent, intellect, discipline and personality of the learners.

My school at a glans

With the spark of a distinguished Military Principal, Gazipur Cantonment Public School & College is in operation to ensure quality education from the nursery level to the college (Nursery to Class Ten - Bangla & English versions, College – Only Bangla Version). The academy with its 137 teachers/staff & around 2500 running students is marked by a group of seasoned coordinators along with highly efficient & energetic teachers graduated from the reputed universities of Bangladesh. One can trace the uniqueness of this institute as class teachers with guide teachers and teachers' assistants with medical assistants incessantly work to uplift the level of competencies inside the students. In the serene greenery around, the campus offers fully-fledged digitalised classrooms, a conference room, students' prayer rooms, well-furnished teachers' rooms attaching prayer rooms & dining halls. The institution, all under CCTV, is already one-step-forward with its online procedures, well-equipped & separate laboratories, language & debating clubs, well-arranged canteen as well as on-time bus services. The teachers are truly alert of the students' athletics as there are ample opportunities for indoor & outdoor sports arranged from time to time. With a harmonious development of mind & intelligentsia, the academia is spreading a brand through reanimating the students' thoughts, lifestyles and professionalism.
